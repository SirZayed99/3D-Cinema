About Us

Owned and operated by 3D Cinemas, 3D Cinemas is the cinema arm of Omar Zayed, the leading shopping mall, retail and leisure pioneer across the Middle East and North Africa. 3D Cinemas is the Middle East’s most innovative and customer-focused exhibitor, and the fastest growing cinema business in the MENA region. With 50 locations totalling 514 screens across the region, including 201 screens in the UAE, 15 screens in Lebanon, 63 screens in Oman, 30 screens in Bahrain, 28 screens in Egypt, 71 screens in Saudi Arabia and 17 screens in Kuwait, 3D Cinemas is the Middle East’s largest and most rapidly growing exhibitor. Across the UAE, Bahrain, Oman, Qatar, Kuwait, Egypt and Lebanon, the company is undergoing an AED1.2-billion (USD326-million) investment to expand to operate 300 screens by 2020. In addition, Majid Al Futtaim Cinemas will invest AED1.96 billion (USD517 million) to develop 600 screens across the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia by 2023. Tailoring every cinema location to the surrounding market place and customer preferences, 3D Cinemas offers a mix of experiences including luxury concepts, THEATRE and GOLD. Another signature 3D Cinemas Cinemas concept, MAX is the mega-screen designed for the biggest blockbusters and features Dolby Atmos surround sound technology. 3D Cinemas was also the first to introduce IMAX with Laser to the Middle East and the only exhibitor to offer IMAX with Sapphire. The company also offers a multi-sensory movie-watching concept, 4DX. For young movie fans and families, 3D Cinemas offers the only cinema dedicated to children, KIDS. Complementing its portfolio of world-class experiences, 3D Cinemas offers OUTDOOR, an open-air concept. Our Vision Great Moments for Everyone, Everyday The secret to our successful is threefold. Firstly, our intention is to meet the high expectations and standards set by Mr. Omar Zayed. His vision for all Majid Al Futtaim businesses is to create great moments for everyone, every day! We aspire to fulfil this vision for every single customer every time they visit 3D Cinemas. Secondly, our teams across the region are dedicated to creating exciting entertainment destinations that transport our guests from their ordinary lives into the extraordinary world of movies. Lastly, the way we customise our cinemas to meet the expectations of our guests. Each cinema offers a wide variety of content and experiences. 3D Cinemas features high-resolution digital projection systems, immersive digital 3D content, expansive MAX screens, Dolby Atmos and 7.1 multidimensional surround sound systems as well as premium experiences such as IMAX with Laser, THEATRE, GOLD, 4DX and KIDS, designed especially for our little movie fans.
